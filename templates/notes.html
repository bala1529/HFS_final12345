Notes

URL Checker

  • Enter a full link (including http:// or https://) in the text box.
  • Click Analyze with no file selected.
  • The system checks the URL using VirusTotal and other heuristics and returns a risk score and explanation.

SMS Checker

  • Paste suspicious SMS / WhatsApp / email text in the box and click Analyze.
  • Risk is based on patterns commonly seen in phishing, lottery scams, fake job offers, etc.
  • You can also upload a screenshot and choose SMS CHECKER from the image options; HFS runs OCR first and then analyses the extracted text.

AI Voice Analyser

  • Upload an audio file (.wav, .mp3, .m4a, etc.).
  • The model estimates spoof / deepfake likelihood and shows a risk score.
  • Use this as an extra signal alongside caller‑ID and common‑sense checks.

Deepfake Image Detector

  • Select an image file and choose DEEPFAKE in the image options.
  • The system runs multiple models to estimate if the face or scene might be AI‑generated or manipulated.
  • The result page shows the input image, risk score, and model explanation (and heatmaps when available).

Deepfake Video Detector

  • Upload a video file (.mp4, .mov, etc.).
  • HFS samples frames across the clip, analyses them, and averages the predictions.
  • The result page lists frame‑by‑frame probabilities plus an overall risk and, when possible, a heatmap.

Feedback

  • Open the Feedback tab from the menu.
  • Provide a valid email address and a short message.
  • Your message is sent to the HFS team for review; do not include passwords or sensitive financial data.
